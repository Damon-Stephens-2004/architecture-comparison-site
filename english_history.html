Historical Development in England

Englands architectural story has layers of history. from Roman occupation and Norman conquests to the rise of Gothic and Tudor styles. Each period introduced distinct design elements, such as Romanesque arches, pointed Gothic windows, and half-timbered houses.

Monuments like Westminster Abbey and Windsor Castle illustrate how English architecture evolved to serve religious, political, and residential needs. The Industrial Revolution later brought new materials like cast iron and glass, pushing English architecture into modernity..
